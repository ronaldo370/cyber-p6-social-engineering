How to spot this phishing attempt:

🚩 Sense of Urgency

Attackers use deadlines like "Friday EOD" to rush you into making mistakes without verifying the source.

🚩 Suspicious URL

Always verify the address bar. Real portals use certified domains. Locally hosted or misspelled IPs are major red flags.

🚩 Unsecured Connection

Always look for the padlock icon. Never enter credentials on a site that lacks HTTPS encryption.
